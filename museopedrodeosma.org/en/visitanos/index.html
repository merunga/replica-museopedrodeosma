VISIT US

Learning, curiosity and fun! The educational programs at the Museo Pedro de Osma are geared towards school groups of all ages and learning backgrounds, as well as college and graduate level students interested in learning more about viceregal culture and art.

Each visit is especially designed to motivate students’ passion for art, and highlight the unique cultural syncretism manifested in viceregal works – a topic that constitutes a fundamental theme in art history.  The education programs ultimately encourage the recognition and appreciation of difference in Peruvian culture.
